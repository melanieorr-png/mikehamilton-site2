National Skills Agreement (NSA): VET Funding, RTO Strategy & Delivery

The National Skills Agreement (NSA) is the Australian Government’s primary funding framework for vocational education and training (VET). It outlines how funding is distributed across states and territories, and sets national priorities for workforce development.

Why the NSA Matters for RTOs and Businesses

  • Access to government funding opportunities
  • Alignment with national workforce priorities
  • Support for Closing the Gap initiatives
  • Focus on priority industries and skills shortages

Key Focus Areas

  • Fee-Free TAFE and subsidised training
  • Centres of Excellence
  • Foundation skills and workforce participation
  • Regional and remote training delivery
